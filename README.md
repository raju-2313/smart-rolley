# Smart Trolley 🛒

A self-checkout web application built for retail stores to cut down billing queues and give customers a faster, smoother shopping experience.

Live Demo → [smarttrolley05.netlify.app](https://smarttrolley05.netlify.app/)

---

## What It Does

Long checkout lines are a real problem in large stores. Smart Trolley lets customers scan products directly into their trolley, track their total in real time, and pay — all without waiting at a counter.

The system runs on a tablet mounted to the trolley and handles the full shopping session from scan to payment confirmation.

---

## Features

- **Barcode Scanning** — Scan products and see them added to the cart instantly
- **Live Cart Updates** — Quantities and totals update on every scan
- **Flexible Payment** — Customers can pay online or head to the counter
- **QR Code Checkout** — A QR code is auto-generated at checkout for quick digital payment
- **Store Map** — Aisle navigation built in for future use
- **Multi-language Support** — English, Hindi, and Marathi
- **Admin Panel** — Separate login for store management
- **Session Management** — Each trolley runs its own session, cleared after checkout

## How to Use (Customer Flow)

1. Check that the cart number on screen matches your trolley
2. Go to the **Billing** page
3. Scan each item and wait for it to show up in the cart
4. When done, tap **Checkout** and pick your payment method
5. After payment goes through, tap **Finish Transaction**
