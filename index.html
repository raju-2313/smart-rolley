ABOUT THE SYSTEM

Smart Trolley

Smart Trolley is a self-checkout retail solution designed to eliminate long billing queues in large stores.

Customers can scan products directly into the trolley, view real-time totals, and proceed to either online payment or counter checkout.

The system is built using modern web technologies and is optimised for tablet-based retail environments.

KEY FEATURES

What makes it powerful

Real-time Barcode Scanning Instant product recognition with live cart updates
Dynamic Cart Management Add, adjust and remove items on the fly
Online & Offline Payment Flexible checkout — digital or counter
QR-based Payment Auto-generated QR for frictionless pay
Store Mapping Integration Future-ready aisle navigation built in
HOW TO USE

Getting started in 5 steps

  1. 01
    Verify the cart number

    Check that the cart number on the display matches the one on your trolley before starting.

  2. 02
    Open Billing

    Navigate to the Billing section on the display screen.

  3. 03
    Scan your items

    Scan each product and wait for it to appear in your cart before moving to the next.

  4. 04
    Checkout & pay

    When done shopping, tap Checkout and select your preferred payment method.

  5. 05
    Finish transaction

    After payment is confirmed, tap Finish Transaction to complete your session.
